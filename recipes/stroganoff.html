Bodacious Beef Stroganoff

Description

This is the most delicious beef stroganoff in all the land.

Ingredients
  • Ribeye
  • Sour Cream
  • Shrooms
  • Worcestershire
  • Egg noodles
  • Parsley
  • Love
  • Time
Steps
  1. Brown that beautiful beef. Don't you dare overcook it.
  2. Sautee onions and garlic in that beef juice.
  3. Add cream and butter and Worcestershire and love.
  4. Slather upon egg noodles and nomnom.
